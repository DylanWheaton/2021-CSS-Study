The 501st Legion, originally operating as an elite group known as the 501st Clone Battalion, was a Republic Military battalion and later legion of elite clone troopers under the command of Jedi General Anakin Skywalker and Jedi Commander Ahsoka Tano. The legion was briefly and tentatively led by Jedi General Pong Krell in one instance that Skywalker was absent. Clone Captain CT-7567 "Rex" served as Skywalker's first-in-command and was eventually promoted to the military rank of Clone Commander when the 501st Legion split into two groups.
The 501st would later go onto to be Vader's personal legion and they would continue to serve under the republic now the empire at the time.
